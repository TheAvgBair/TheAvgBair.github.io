Level.io Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-30

Level.io is designed to keep your tasks, habits, character progress, inventory, and settings on your device unless you choose to export, sync, or share them.

Information The App Stores

Level.io may store the following on your device:

  • Tasks, recurring habits, completion history, streaks, XP, gold, health, stamina, gear, skill choices, character customization, and app settings.
  • Optional local backups of your app data.
  • Local diagnostic breadcrumbs used to help recover from crashes or data issues.
  • Hashed duplicate-detection fingerprints used to reduce XP farming. These are not stored as readable task words.

Optional Apple Reminders Sync

Reminders sync is off by default. If you enable it, Level.io asks for Apple Reminders access and can import incomplete reminders as quests. Completing or deleting imported quests may update or delete the matching Apple Reminder.

Optional iCloud Backup

Cloud backup is off by default. If you enable it, Level.io can save encrypted backup snapshots to your private iCloud/CloudKit storage so you can restore app data later. These backups are tied to your Apple ID and private iCloud account.

Optional Diagnostics And Feedback

Diagnostics sharing is off by default. If you choose to share a report, Level.io prepares a local report with app version, build, device/OS context, crash status, and sanitized diagnostic entries. You control whether that report is copied or shared.

Backup Export And Import

You can export and import backup JSON files from Settings. Exported backups may contain your tasks, habits, character progress, and game state. You are responsible for where you save or send exported files.

Tracking And Advertising

This build does not track you across apps or websites and does not include third-party advertising SDKs.

Data Sharing

Level.io does not sell your data. The app does not upload tasks, habits, or diagnostics to a developer-hosted server in this build. Data can leave your device only when you choose an optional Apple service, export/share a backup, or share a diagnostic report.

Contact

For privacy questions, support, or feedback, contact TheAvgDevs@gmail.com.
