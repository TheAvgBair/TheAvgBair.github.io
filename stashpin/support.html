StashPin Support

Thanks for using StashPin. If something does not work as expected, send a message and we will take a look.

Contact

Email support at:

TheAvgDevs@gmail.com

What To Include

Please include as much of this as you can:

  • StashPin version and build number.
  • iPhone model and iOS version.
  • What you were trying to do.
  • What happened.
  • What you expected to happen.
  • Whether the issue involves items, locations, tags, photos, search, backup, restore, sharing, privacy settings, or app icons.
  • Whether the issue involves StashPin Unlimited, restoring purchases, or subscription status.

Before Troubleshooting

StashPin stores data locally on your device. If you need to preserve your data before troubleshooting, create a backup from Settings > Backup & Restore.

Privacy

For details about how StashPin handles app data, read the privacy policy.
