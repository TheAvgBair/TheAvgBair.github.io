StashPin

Support and privacy information for StashPin, a local-first app for remembering where your stored items live.

Get Support Privacy Policy

Local First

StashPin stores items, locations, notes, tags, photos, settings, and backups on your device.

No Tracking

The app does not include accounts, ads, analytics, tracking SDKs, or developer-hosted cloud sync.

Backups

You can create backup files from inside the app and choose where to save or share them.
