StashPin Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-23

StashPin is designed as a local-first personal inventory app. It stores your items, locations, notes, tags, photos, app settings, and backups on your device.

StashPin does not collect data on developer servers. The app does not include analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, accounts, or developer-hosted cloud sync.

Information Stored On Your Device

StashPin may store the following locally on your device:

  • Item names, locations, notes, tags, alternate names, important/private flags, and movement history.
  • Photos you choose through the system photo picker.
  • Local app settings such as appearance, haptics, privacy visibility, and app icon choice.
  • Backup files you create or restore through the app.

Photos

When you choose photos through the system photo picker, StashPin copies those selected photos into local app storage so they can appear with your saved items and locations. StashPin only accesses photos you select.

Backups, Exports, And Sharing

You can create backups, export data, and share item details using iOS sharing tools. Those actions are initiated by you. Exports can include item names, locations, notes, tags, dates, privacy flags, and photos when you choose to include them.

You are responsible for where you save or send exported backup files.

Tracking And Advertising

StashPin does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting services. StashPin does not sell your data.
